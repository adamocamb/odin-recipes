Description

Curry is one of my favorite dishes. This recipe is out of a cookbook from Yui's cooking class.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup coconut cream
  • 2 tbsp green curry paste
  • 150 g chicken breast
  • 1 1/2 cups coconut milk
  • 200 g eggplant (bite size pieces)
  • 20 g small eggplant (optional)
  • 2-3 kaffir lime leaves (torn in quarters)
  • 2 tsp palm sugar
  • 1-1 1/2 tbsp fish sauce (be careful!)
  • large red chili (thin sliced)
  • 40 g sweet basil leaves

Steps

  1. boil the coconut cream over low heat until the surface looks shiny and oil appears.
  2. Add the curry paste and cook for 1 minute or until fregrant.
  3. Add chicken and cook for 1 minute then add the coconut milk.
  4. Bring to a boil. Add the eggplant, small eggplant, and kaffir lime leaves.
  5. After the eggplant becomes soft, season with palm sugar, fish sauce, and add chili.
  6. Add the sweet basil leaves at the last moment and stir. Remove from heat.

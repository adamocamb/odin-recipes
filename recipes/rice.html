Mango Sticky Rice

Description

This is a simple and tasty dessert dish. It's just sticky rice with coconut and sugar, with a sweet coconut sauce and mango on top. This recipe is from Yui's cookbook.

Ingredients

Mango Sticky Rice

  • 1 cup sticky rice or glutinous rice (soaked for 6 hours)
  • 1 cup very thick coconut cream
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 pinches salt

Sauce

  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 2 pinches salt
  • 1/4 cup coconut cream
  • 1 mango (peeled and cut in chunks)

Steps

Mango Sticky Rice

  1. Drain the rice and steam over medium heat for about 15 minutes.
  2. Remove the rice from the steamer and mix with the coconut cream, sugar, and salt immediately. Stir gently.
  3. Cover and let stand for about 1/2 hour.

Sauce

  1. Mix all ingredients together.
  2. Bring to a boil over low heat.
  3. Keep stirring until sugar is dissolved.
  4. Remove from heat.
  5. Pour on top of sticky rice before serving. Serve with mango.
